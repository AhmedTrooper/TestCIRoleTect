🔍 How to Find Tags (DevTools)

1. Open Inspector: Right-click any part of a webpage and select "Inspect" (or press F12).

2. Find the Code: Hover over the elements in the inspector. The part of the page they belong to will highlight in blue.

3. Copy ID or Class: Look for id="..." (use #) or class="..." (use .).

⌨️ Selector Cheatsheet
#main-content

Targets a unique ID. Best for stability.

.job-description

Targets a Class. Great for content blocks.

.text.px-3.pt-2

Targets Multiple Classes on one tag (join with dots).

#title, #desc

Targets Multiple Elements at once (join with comma).

🚀 Advanced Mode Setup

Go to Settings and add your favorite sites. For example, add "LinkedIn" with selector .jobs-description. Now you can just select LinkedIn from the dropdown in the Extract tab!
